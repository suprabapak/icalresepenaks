Pineanna Nut Cake

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 262 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 264 reviews
Yield : 9

Ingredients

  • 3 1/3 c. Softasilk cake flour
  • 2 c. sugar
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1 c. vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 4 medium ripe bananas, chopped
  • 1 c. chopped pecans
  • 1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 8 oz. can crushed pineapple with juice

Directions

  • Heat oven to 350u00b0.
  • Grease and flour 3 round cake pans, 9 x 1 1/2-inches. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, salt and cinnamon in large bowl.
  • Add oil and eggs.
  • Stir until all dry ingredients are moistened; do not beat.
  • Stir in bananas, 1 cup pecans, the vanilla and pineapple.
  • Divide batter evenly among pans.
  • Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  • Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans.
  • Cool completely on wire racks.
  • Fill layer and frost side and top of cake with cream cheese frosting.
  • Sprinkle 1/2 cup of pecans on top of cake.
  • Cut cake using sharp knife.
  • Refrigerate any remaining cake.
