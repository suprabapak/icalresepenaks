Dirt Dessert

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 291 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 167 reviews
Yield : 5

Ingredients

  • 2 8 oz. cream cheese, melted
  • 1 stick butter, melted
  • 2 small instant vanilla pudding
  • 3 c. milk
  • 1 c. 10x sugar
  • 1 large Cool Whip
  • 1 large pkg. Oreo cookies

Directions

  • Put Oreo cookies in blender to make them look like dirt.
  • Set them aside.
  • Cream the cream cheese and butter.
  • Add pudding, sugar and milk, then fold in the Cool Whip.
  • In a large container, put a line of the dirt, then the pudding until you reach the top with the dirt on top.
