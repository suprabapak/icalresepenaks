Microwave Lasagna

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 256 calories,
3.9 stars - based on 55 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 32 oz. jar spaghetti sauce
  • Italian seasoning
  • 1 large clove garlic or 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 c. water
  • 1 8 oz. pkg. lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 lb. extra lean ground beef
  • 3 c. grated Jack cheese
  • 1 c. Ricotta cheese
  • 1 c. grated Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 c. Parmesan cheese

Directions

  • Mix first four ingredients.
  • Layer in 9 x 13-inch casserole thin layer of sauce, 3 raw noodles, all of raw ground beef, 1/2 Jack cheese, 1/3 sauce, 3 noodles, spread all Ricotta cheese, rest of Jack cheese, 1/2 remaining sauce, 3 noodles and rest of sauce. Cover loosely.
  • Cook on 1/2 power 5 10 minutes, turn 1/4.
  • Cook 1/2 power 10 minutes.
  • Let stand 5 minutes.
  • Turn 1/4, cook on High 10 7 minutes.
  • Add cheese Mozzarella and Parmesan.
  • Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
