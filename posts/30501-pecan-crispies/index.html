Pecan Crispies

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 194 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 115 reviews
Yield : 5

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. shortening
  • 1/2 c. butter/oleo
  • 2 1/2 c. brown sugar
  • 2 well beaten eggs
  • 3 c. flour
  • 1/2 tsp. soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 c. chopped pecans

Directions

  • Cream shortening, butter, sugar and eggs.
  • Beat well dry ingredients, add to creamed mixture and add nuts. Drop by tsp. about 2 inches apart. Bake at 350u00b0 for 12-15 minutes. Makes 5 dozen.
