Microwave Fat-Free Risotto

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 191 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 33 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. 120 ml minced yellow onion about 1/4 lb., 113.5 g
  • 1 c. 240 ml arborio rice
  • 3 c. 705 ml Basic Chicken Broth or unsalted or regular canned chicken broth
  • 1/4 c. 60 ml chopped fresh herbs optional
  • pinch of cayenne pepper

Directions

  • Place onions in an 11 x 8 1/2 x 2-inch 28 x 21.5 x 5-cm dish. Cook, uncovered, at 100% for 2 minutes in a 650 to 700 watt oven. Stir in rice and cook, uncovered, for 1 minute or until rice is milky-white, but not browned.
