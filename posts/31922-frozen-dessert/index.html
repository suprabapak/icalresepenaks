Frozen Dessert

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 165 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 45 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 1 angel food cake
  • 1 small pkg. orange jello
  • 1 small pkg. lime jello
  • 1 small pkg. strawberry jello
  • 1 large can mandarin oranges, well drained
  • 1 can blueberries, drained
  • 2 pkg. frozen strawberries, thawed
  • 3/4 gal. vanilla ice cream

Directions

  • Divide cake into 3 sections.
  • Break into pieces about large marshmallow size.
  • Place in tube pan 1/3 of cake with dry orange jello.
  • Place oranges on top, then spread 1/3 of ice cream over all.
  • Mix 1/3 of cake pieces with dry lime jello, then add to pan. Cover with blueberries followed by 1/3 of ice cream.
  • Mix last 1/3 of cake with dry strawberry jello and add to pan, followed by strawberries and last of ice cream.
  • Freeze at least 3 days.
