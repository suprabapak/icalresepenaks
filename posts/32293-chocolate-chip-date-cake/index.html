Chocolate Chip Date Cake

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 252 calories,
4 stars - based on 64 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients

  • 1 c. chopped dates
  • 1 1/2 c. boiling water
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 c. shortening
  • 1 c. sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/4 c. flour
  • 1/4 tsp. soda baking
  • 1 pkg. chocolate chips
  • 1/2 c. nut meats
  • 1/2 c. sugar

Directions

  • Pour boiling water over dates and 1 teaspoon baking soda; let cool.
  • Cream shortening and sugar.
  • Add beaten eggs, then add cooled mixture.
  • Sift together the flour and 1/4 teaspoon soda; add to creamed mixture.
  • Mix well.
  • Pour into a 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan that has been greased and floured.
  • Mix the last 3 ingredients; spread on top of cake.
  • Bake at 350u00b0 for 30 to 35 minutes.
